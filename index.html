This website is under construction. You can see my old website: here.

I am working as a Postdoctoral researcher with Professor Ivan Viola in the Nanovisualization Research Group, KAUST, Saudi Arabia.

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR) , Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) , under the supervision of Prof. Xiaopeng Zhang and Prof. Dong-Ming Yan . Soon after completing my Ph.D., I worked as an Assistant Professor with Prof. Hirokazu Kato (the inventor of ARToolKit) at the IMD-Interactive Media Design Lab, Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST), Japan.

My research interests include Computer Graphics, Visualization, Virtual Reality, 3D Deep Learning, and Human-AI interactions.
